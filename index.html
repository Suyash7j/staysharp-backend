00 — What is this

Built for and by the person who keeps almost starting.

Stay Sharp is a personal accountability system for three non-negotiable daily habits: reading, soft skills, and a side project.

Log every session. See your streaks. Get a shame notification — WhatsApp, push, or email — if you don't show up before your deadline.

No coaching. No community. Just you and the log.

01 — The three reps
01
📚
Book Reading
Log sessions, pages, key takeaways. Track your streak. Build a library of what you've actually absorbed.
02
🗣
Soft Skills
Vocal drills, communication practice, decision making. The skills everyone needs but nobody trains daily.
03
💻
Side Project
Log your task, time, and what you shipped or unblocked. Consistency over intensity. Always.
02 — How it works
01
Open the tracker each day

The interface resets every morning. Your streak history persists. Your excuses don't.

02
Log each habit with details

Time spent, notes, reflections. Logging is the habit — writing it down is the commitment.

03
Set your deadline

8 PM, 10 PM, or midnight. Whatever you choose, the system holds you to it.

04
Get called out if you miss

WhatsApp message, push notification, and email to your accountability partner. No hiding.

05
Review your dashboard

Streaks, weekly heatmap, hours logged, consistency rate. See the shape of your discipline.
